# Acme Corp Employee Handbook

## 1. Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Approval from a direct manager is required by Wednesday of the preceding week.

## 2. Vacation Time
Standard vacation allowance is 20 days per year for full-time employees. Unused days can carry over to the next year, but a maximum of 5 days can be carried over.

## 3. Reimbursements
Travel expenses are reimbursed if submitted within 60 days of the expense. Receipts are mandatory for any item over $25.

## 4. Health and Safety
Acme Corp provides a $500 annual stipend for home office ergonomic equipment. This stipend refreshes every January 1st.
